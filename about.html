About Benito a.k.a Bad Bunny

Benito Antonio Martinez Ocasio, was born and raised in Vega Baja, Puerto Rico. Having always had an interest in music his first music performance was in middle school where he sang Juanes's song "Mala Gente". He came up with his stage name looking back at image where he was forced to wear a bunny costome to school and appeared to be upset in the photo, thus giving himself the name 'Bad Bunny'.

In 2013, Bad Bunny began to upload his songs on the music app SoundCloud. It wasn't until 2016 where he got the attention of DJ Luian for his song Diles. This song got him signed on to Dj Luian's Label "Hear This Music". From there, he continued to release singles such as "Soy Peor", and "Krippy Kush". Both of these singles were successful and so were his future singles "Ahora me llama", with Karol G, and "Mayores", with Becky G, and most recognized "Te bote". In 2017, Bad Bunny's name could be found on 15 songs on the Billboard Hot Latin Chart. In 2018, Bad Bunny's fame only continued to rise. Bad Bunny collaborated with artists such as Cardi B, and Drake. The song "I Like It", sang with Cardi B and J Balvin, was Bad Bunny's first song on the US Billboard Hot 100.

Soon after leaving his label "Hear This Music", Bad Bunny revealed that the reason as to why he has not released an album was because he was not allowed to do so, even though he produced all of his songs. So, after leaving the label Bad Bunny released his first album, "x100pre" under Rimas Entertainment. The album received a rating of 84 on Metacritic, and later was placed on Rolling Stone's industry-voted list of the 500 Greatest Albums of All Time at number 447.

On June 2019, Bad Bunny released a joint album with J Balvin names "Oasis". Despite being a suprise album, it reached number 9 on Billboard Hot 100.

In February of 2020, Bad Bunny released "YHLQMDLG" which stands for "Yo Hago Lo Que Me Da La Gana" or "I Do Whatever I Want". This album became the highest charting Spanish album on the US Billboard 200. In this album, one of the most important songs was called "Yo Perreo Sola". It was this song where Bad Bunna calls out sexual harrasment of women. It was also this song that bbecame Bad Bunny's 9th No.1 song in just only two years.

On April 2020, during the beginning of the pandemic that swept the globe, Bad Bunny decided to release an album comprising of songs that had did not made the cute on his previous albums. Thus the title of the album was "Los Que No Iban Salir", which translates to "The Ones That Weren't Going to Come Out". During this time, Bad Bunny and YHLQMDLG became spotify's most streamed artist and album.

Despite having already released 2 albums in 2020, Bad Bunny released another album, "El Ultimo Tour Del Mundo", or "The Last Tour of the World". This album was just as successful, if not more, thatn "YHLQMDLG". The album was the first latin album that reached number 1 on the Billboard 200. The album was anticipated as in his last official album, "YHLQMDLG", on the last track Bad Bunny stated in the lyrics that in 9 months he will come out with his last album and then retire. That statment was a lie.

After the success of the last album, Bad Bunny changed his style of music and released "Un Verano Sin Ti" in May of 2022. This album debuted at number 1 on the Billboard 200, making it his second number-one album.
